At PearFiction I was tasked with the development of web browser based real-money gambling games. Responsibilities included coordination of a small development team, creating prototypes and products from specifications, making technical decisions, running code reviews, and general quality assurance tasks.

My most notable accomplishment was leading the development of an in-house game engine used to build their browser based slot-machine games. This provided the studio with a powerful set of graphics tools to rapidly prototype and develop any sort of rich multimedia slot-machine experience they could envision.
